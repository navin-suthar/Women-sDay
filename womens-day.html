Happy Women's Day

A Hinglish Poem — Dil Se

Tu phool hai, tu aandhi bhi hai,
Tu nazuk hai, tu fauladi bhi hai.
She blooms like a rose in every storm,
Har mushkil mein rehti hai warm.

Ghar sambhaale, duniya bhi banaaye,
Dreams ko apne kabhi na bhulaaye.
She wears her scars like golden art,
Toot ke bhi, jodti hai har dil ka part.

Maa hai, beti hai, dost bhi hai,
Har role mein best wo hoti hai.
She carries the world on her gentle hands,
Phir bhi apni udaan ko wo kehti — "I can!"

Tujhe salaam hai, aye naari,
Tu hai is duniya ki mehndaari.
Not just today, but every single day,
Teri himmat ko hum sab — salute karte hain aaj!

So rise, shine, aur khul ke jio,
Apni kahani khud hi likhte jao.
The world is yours — tu hai capable,
Happy Women's Day — you are remarkable! 🌹
